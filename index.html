A web designer is a professional responsible for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. They combine creative and technical skills to design the layout, graphics, and overall look and feel of a website. Web designers work with various tools and technologies, including graphic design software, HTML, CSS, and sometimes JavaScript, to bring their designs to life on the web. Their primary goal is to ensure that websites are aesthetically pleasing, easy to navigate, and responsive across different devices. Web designers often collaborate with web developers to turn their designs into functional websites, and they play a crucial role in creating a positive user experience for visitors to a site.
